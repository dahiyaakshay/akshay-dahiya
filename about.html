Path

How I got here_

I began with SEO to understand how visibility is engineered. Then expanded into paid acquisition and performance media because growth is never just organic or paid — it's systemic. Over time, I went deeper into GA4, GTM, and attribution modeling to eliminate guesswork and measure what actually drives revenue.

Working inside agency environments exposed me to scale — multiple industries, multiple funnels, multiple budgets. Patterns became clear. The same structural problems kept repeating: weak tracking, disconnected tools, reactive decision-making. That’s where I shifted from executing tactics to building repeatable growth systems.

Moving into freelance and product consulting meant owning the full stack — from high-performance websites to conversion architecture across WordPress, Framer, and custom builds. Not just design, but speed, structure, and measurable outcomes. Traffic without control is noise. I wanted leverage.

The deeper issue wasn’t strategy — it was execution fragmentation. Teams drowning in disconnected tools. Context switching. Lost insight. So I started building internal engines — automation layers, tracking frameworks, intelligence dashboards. That foundation evolved into MarAI, RetrieveAI, and RankScan — a unified AI ecosystem built to control creation, retrieval visibility, and performance intelligence.

Case Study

Why I Built The AI Intelligence Stack_

I kept seeing the same problem: teams juggling 10+ AI and marketing tools — one for content, one for SEO, one for research, another for analytics. The issue wasn’t lack of AI. It was fragmentation. Context switching kills execution speed.

Real growth doesn’t happen in silos. Creation, strategy, retrieval visibility, and performance intelligence need to operate as one system. But most platforms solve narrow problems, forcing teams into disconnected workflows.

So I built an integrated AI stack: MarAI for marketing asset creation and workflow execution, RetrieveAI for AI retrieval and citation intelligence, and RankScan for multi-provider visibility analytics. Built on Node.js + PostgreSQL, powered by Google Gemini and Perplexity AI.

I didn’t build these to chase trends. I built them to eliminate tool sprawl, unify execution, and give marketers control over how AI creates, retrieves, and represents their brand.
